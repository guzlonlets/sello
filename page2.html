About us

We are the only gutter company in South Africa that manufactures stainless steel, galvanized steel and colour material. Sello’s Gutters Manufacturing is 100% black owned, level 1 BEE and holds the trade mark for the most sort after fascia boards. Sello’s fascias offers a unique look to any house or development.

Sello’s Gutters strives to deliver good quality service and products where necessary in the private sector as well as in local government (municipalities), whilst creating a long-term value for all its stakeholders.

Sello’s Gutters manufacturing is striving towards creating jobs for the community as well as becoming a known force throughout South Africa.

Our Mission and Vision

In pursuit of our vision, the company is committed to actively explore and implement value-added products and services and operational participation in:

– Guttering System

– Stainless Steel

In concert with our staff and key stakeholder, Sello’s Gutters aims to improve the effectiveness and effeciency of our organizations. Our approaches are linked to task analysis, appreciation and execution for quality services with after care business services to the satisfaction of our clients. We are committed to long-term partnership with our clients and the provision of comprehensive and cost-effective service in accordance with the client’s requirements. Sello’s Gutters also invests in the intangible assets of intellectual capital, competencies and business networks.

Our mission is to directly supply the end user and offer the best service with a great price and excellent quality. Sello’s Gutters manufacturing is striving towards creating jobs for the community as well as becoming a known force throughout South Africa.

Service Excellence and Quality

Professionalism, service orientation and timeliness in the rendition of service commitment, consistent with its vision and mission, remain the hallmark of the company’s superior service delivery to its most valued assets, its customers.

Sello’s Gutters operates in a dynamic business environment and relies on the diversity and skills that the staff possesses.
